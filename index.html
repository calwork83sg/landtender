📊 Why This Price?

The Residual Land Value method calculates the maximum land price you can pay while still achieving your target returns. It works backwards from projected revenue:

Land Value = Gross Development Value (GDV) − Total Development Cost − Developer Profit Margin − Financing Costs
  • GDV: Present value of all future rental income over the leasehold period
  • Dev Cost: Construction + fees + contingency + marketing
  • Profit Margin: Your required developer margin on total cost
  • Financing: Interest on land & construction loans during development

💡 Tender Strategy: The Base Case gives you a competitive bid that meets your IRR target. Use the Conservative case as your walk-away price, and the Aggressive case only if you have high conviction on occupancy and rental growth.
