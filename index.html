How to interpret

The base residual land value is the maximum tender that still delivers your target equity return, given your rental, operating cost and financing assumptions. You may choose to bid closer to the conservative value if you want more buffer, or nearer the aggressive value if you are comfortable taking more leasing and cost risk.
