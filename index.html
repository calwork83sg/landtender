Singapore IRAS Property Tax Context: Dormitories are taxed at 10% of Annual Value (AV) for non-residential properties. AV is estimated at 5% of gross rental income. Property tax is deductible for income tax purposes. Income tax rate for companies is 17%. Leasehold property returns to government at $0 at expiry.

IRAS Tax Treatment for Dormitories:
• Property Tax: 10% of Annual Value (AV). AV for dormitories estimated at 5% of gross annual rent (non-residential rate).
• Income Tax: 17% corporate tax rate on chargeable income after deducting allowable expenses (interest, OpEx, property tax, depreciation).
• Depreciation: Straight-line over leasehold period or 25 years (building), whichever is shorter. No capital allowances for land.
• Leasehold: Property returns to government at $0 value at expiry. No terminal value.
